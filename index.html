About KMD

A Full-Process Industrial Service Company Founded in 2016

KMD Technology Co., Ltd. is committed to providing professional industrial services to customers worldwide. Our team is composed of senior industry experts with deep experience in the industrial service sector.

Founded in 2016, our business covers four integrated service segments: integrated technical solution consulting, procurement services, project management, and equipment delivery — offering clients a truly seamless, end-to-end experience.

With deep roots in the electric motor and pump industry and strong partnerships with WEG, VEM, and Xylem, we are uniquely positioned to serve the energy, oil & gas, water, HVAC, and industrial sectors across the Middle East and global markets.

Our Service Model

End-to-End Services Across Four Dimensions

KMD's integrated service model ensures that clients receive expert support at every phase of their project — from initial concept through final equipment commissioning.

01
Technical Solution Consulting

Our senior industry experts provide integrated technical consulting — from motor and pump selection, system design, energy efficiency analysis, to application-specific engineering recommendations for oil & gas, water, and HVAC projects.

02
Procurement Services

Leveraging our direct relationships with WEG, VEM, and Xylem, KMD handles the full procurement process — sourcing, vendor qualification, commercial negotiation, purchase order management, and expediting to ensure competitive pricing and on-time supply.

03
Project Management

Dedicated project managers oversee the full project lifecycle — coordinating with manufacturers, managing timelines, handling inspection and factory acceptance tests (FAT), and ensuring complete documentation including certifications and compliance reports.

04
Equipment Delivery

KMD manages the complete export and logistics chain — arranging freight, handling customs clearance, preparing all shipping documentation, and coordinating last-mile delivery to project sites across the GCC, Middle East, and international markets.
